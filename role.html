What is Sent?
It is a function to register a level in the "Rating List" that can be seen by Elder Moders. Elder mods can rate based on this list.

What is Power Rate?
It is a function that produces the "effect of voting for 3 people by 1 vote" when voting for demon face and non-rated level difficulty.
